# Training Course Image Prompts

Visual system for all course imagery.

## Shared Style Rules

- Premium enterprise aesthetic.
- Dark graphite environment (#0d0f12 base, deep charcoal gradients).
- Cinematic lighting with soft purple and copper accents.
- Futuristic technology mood without sci-fi clichés.
- Minimal composition with generous negative space.
- No stock people, no faces, no classroom photos, no real-world desks.
- No cartoon, flat illustration, or emoji-like style.
- Consistent color palette: graphite black, soft violet (#8d51a0), cool blue, warm copper/gold highlights.
- Subtle glass, metal, and light-refraction materials.
- 16:9 landscape orientation, high resolution, print-ready quality.
